# Takedown policy

If you own a site whose content appears here and you want it removed, open an
issue titled **"Takedown: <bundle-name>"** or email the maintainer. We will:

1. Remove the bundle's entry from `registry.yaml` within **48 hours**, and
2. Ask the publisher to unpublish the bundle repo's releases.

We honor takedown requests from content owners regardless of the license
recorded — ownership overrides. No justification is required beyond
identifying yourself as the owner.
